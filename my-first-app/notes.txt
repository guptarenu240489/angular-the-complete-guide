1. If you want to inject a service, within another service, always provice the services inside
app module, else their instance will not be available within service.

2. If you want to inject service inside conponent, they can be provided in app conponent.

3. Same insance of service will travel from its parent to all child component.

4. If you provide a service inside a component, all childs of this component will share same instance
but not parent of this component.

5. Be careful about when and where to provide a service.
